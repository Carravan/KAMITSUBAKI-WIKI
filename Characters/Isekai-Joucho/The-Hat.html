Origin

wqbxcic02r.pngThe Hat is part of Joucho's VWP costume and was revealed on 13/03/2021 during KAF's Q2 concert, alongside the reveal of VWP itself. It quickly gained in popularity on the Discord due to it's, frankly, ridicoulous size, adding to Joucho's refinded yet adorable appearance. Joucho is perceived to have talent in many areas, which has since been attributed to The Hat. Some would go as far as to say that Joucho is actually The Hat, and the human called Joucho just happened to put it on.

Use

The Hat is often used as explanation or justification for things related to KAMITSUBAKI. Various talents and events are attributed to The Hat.

Things The Hat does

The Hat is responsible for any and all spelling mistakes KAMITSUBAKI makes.

The Hat summons illustrators as it pleases.

The real friends are the hats we wore along the way.
