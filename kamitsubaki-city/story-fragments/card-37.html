It would appear my end is drawing close. Right now, I'm inside a church, surrounded by a swarm of manifested shadows. Back against the wall, as they say. The sky dyed pitch black, the number of shadows unfathomable. There is no technique of my knowledge that could even put up a fight anymore. Just like everyone else, I will be devoured without a trace. Afterwards, all that remains will be a ruined, desolate cityscape and a handful of memory fragments. This is the true story behind the memory eggs I've been gathering all this time. There is just one thing still within my power. If this shall be my fate, then may these memoirs carved deep into my heart provide guidance for those that come after me. Such is my wish. Such is the final duty I will carry out. The sole hope left behind by a person that failed to save even a single damn thing...
